Who we are

We are a small team of technically knowledgeable writers led by Toma Kashamov, an expert in the field. We compose, edit, deliver and maintain technical content fot the IT community. Whether you call us technical writers or technical authors, or technical communicators, that's us.

We also like to innovate, improve and organize tech docs for any piece of software, whether intuitive or complex to use. We are tech-savvy but writing is not not the only thing we do. We can also help establish the business processes, content strategy and shape the documentation delivery cycles to fit your needs.

Our mission

We are at an early stage. In the beginning, techwriters.eu will hold a simple portfolio showcasing some CVs, a blog and different approaches to technical docs with example doc portals. Eventually, you will be able to view a variety of products presented and our and the ways we use them to author, publish, store and update technical documentation.

Our way with documentation is: keep it simplified, well-structured and consistent.
