Who we are

We are a small team of professional technical writers led by Toma Kashamov, an expert in the field. We compose, edit, deliver and maintain technical content fot the IT community. Some call us technical authors, others call us technical communicators. Information engineers is another title we've obtained.

No matter how you call us, we like to innovate, experiment and organize tech docs for any piece of software, whether intuitive or complex to use. Being tech-savvy is part of the job but we also know how to establish the end-to-end business processes and fit the documentation delivery cycles to your requirements.

Our mission

We are at an early stage. In the beginning, techwriters.eu will hold a portfolio showcasing different approaches to technical docs. You can see different products and approaches to authoring, publishing, storing and updating technical documentation.

Our way with documentation is: keep it simplified, well-structured and consistent.

We embrace latest technology trends for best results and work with any possible output types you require for your technical content. Whether you wish to refresh your portal, update your existing documentation, or restructure your library, we have the expertise.

We care about other technical writers

Yes, albeit competition, possible disagreements, varieties of technologies and methodologies and approaches and software of preference, we form a strong community. Tech authors are the scholars of the modern world and together combined we form the link between technology (for example, software) and all its users. We constantly create instructions, guides, manuals and books that bridge the gap between humans and the vast range of opportunities that machines may grant us.

Your benefits
